Our agents can execute live payments including bulk payments on business accounts to test your Open Banking Payments product and services.

Another very simple use case. You request us to make a payment using your Open Banking App (or by clicking on an Authorisation URL), we make the payment and then you pay us back (including a fee) once you are happy with our service.
